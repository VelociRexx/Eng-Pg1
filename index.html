Movie adaptations of treasured novels or comics are very difficult to pull off. There are many adaptations from novels to movies that do the change well, incorporating the themes of the novel into the movie and creating a successful product as a result. Other movies change too many things, or just don’t create an entertaining enough story, and leave the audiences disappointed with the end product. A huge part of adapting written stories to movies is keeping the themes from the source. Keeping the novel’s themes can almost guarantee a success from the fans. Dropping the themes to basically create your own story with an already existing IP will only destroy the chances of making a successful movie.
